Who are we?

Rollntrip was founded by a water sports enthusiast who has spent a lot of time traveling and meeting local communities worldwide, always searching for new surfing spots.

That's why we are committed to bridging the gap between local experts and travelers, so everyone can discover new ways to enjoy the waves and have their best moments riding it.

Our concept

Rollntrip brings the thrill of e-foiling straight to Europe’s scenic coastline—from Saint-Tropez and the French Riviera to the rest of the world.

  • We come to you – Ride at top beaches like Saint-Raphael, Cannes, or even your private villa.
  • Book instantly – Scan a QR code on flyers or social media.
  • Ride prime times – Morning & afternoon sessions.

We plan to expand further in 2025.
